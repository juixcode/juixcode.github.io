Vanilla Hammers

Overview

Vanilla Hammers Datapack adds craftable and working hammers in Minecraft that mine 3x3 blocks. It requires a resourcepack to enable custom hammers textures.

Advantages

✔ For 3x3 Mining
✔ Multidirectional (up, down, front)
✔ Can be enchanted as a normal pickaxe
✔ Can't break obsidian & bedrock

Disadvantages

✖ Doesn't work with Fortune and Silk Touch enchantments
✖ Resourcepack required
